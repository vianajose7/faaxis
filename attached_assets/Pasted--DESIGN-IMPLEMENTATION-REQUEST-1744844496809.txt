‑ Overall vibe: Fin‑tech / trading dashboard, high contrast, sleek.
‑ Color  palette:
    -- bg‑gradient‑top:  #0D0F18
    -- bg‑gradient‑bottom: #121424
    -- primary‑accent:   #755CFF  (lavender‑indigo)

    -- text‑primary:     #FFFFFF

‑ Font:  “Inter” 400 / 600; fallback “Roboto”, sans‑serif.

  (Framer Motion; 0.4 s easeOut).
